Software Engineering

Software engineering

The Software Engineering educational program will allow you to gain fundamental and applied competencies, including
knowledge and skills in the following areas:
– design and evaluation of algorithms and data structures;
– development and analysis of application software systems using modern programming languages;
– development and analysis of interacting processes in information environments;
– testing, quality assessment and management of the software creation process;
– working with machine learning algorithms and using cloud and high-performance computing.

Career Opportunities

– Engineer-programmer;
– Software developer;
– Back-end and front-end developer;
– Web developer;
– Software tester;
– Manager and architect of it systems and projects;
– Mobile developer.

The goal of the course

The purpose of the “Software Engineering” educational program is to intensively teach students the theoretical
and practical aspects of software development, as well as improve their skills in related industries, such as
mathematics, project management and entrepreneurship. After students gain a degree, they will be qualified to
work as junior / middle developers in many sectors of the economy, including communications, finance, healthcare,
manufacturing, management, and so on.
